Policy

Shipping & Delivery

Processing Time

Each piece is prepared and quality-checked prior to dispatch. Orders are processed within 2–4 business days of payment confirmation.

Delivery Times

  • Domestic (US): 3–6 business days
  • Canada & UK: 5–10 business days
  • Europe & Australia: 7–14 business days
  • Rest of world: 10–21 business days

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to customs processing, which is outside our control.

Shipping Costs

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on destination and order value. Any customs duties or import taxes for international orders are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Tracking

You will receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order ships.

Questions

For any shipping inquiry, contact us at concierge@aurele.luxury.
