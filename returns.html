Policy

Returns & Exchanges

Return Window

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

How to Start a Return

Email concierge@aurele.luxury with your order reference and the items you'd like to return. We'll respond with instructions and a return address.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Made-to-order or altered pieces
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Gift cards

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived defective or incorrect.

Exchanges

For a different size or piece, we recommend returning the original item for a refund and placing a new order to ensure availability.
